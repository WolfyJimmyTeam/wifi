  2. Wireless

Use the buttons to quickly and easily set up your wireless router.

To set up WiFi:

  • SSID: The network name or SSID is a unique name that identifies the wireless network. Wi-Fi devices automatically detect all networks within range. Assign an identification string of up to 32 characters for your wireless connection.
  • Key: This field requires a password of 8 and 63 characters (letters, numbers or a combination) or 64 hex digits to start the encryption process.
  • 2.4GHz (5GHz) Guest: The guest WiFi can temporarily provide 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz network connections. Guest can to the specific network name (SSID) and won't connect to your private network.
